Tower of Hanoi Game

The Tower of Hanoi is a mathematical game/puzzle consisting of 3 rods and a number of disks which comes in various sizes that can slide into any rod. Basic rule is you can transfer any disk to any rod but the disk underneath it must be a bigger size disk than the one going on top of it. Main objective is to transfer the whole stack on a different rod than where you started while following the basic rule.
